ABOUT US...

Tekhne supplies was made for anyone who wants to let their imagination fly. We want everyone to feel comfortable and creative once they looked over all the options of art supplies we have, as well as school and journalist supplies!


Like the greek word to art, we decided to name our web site Tekhne, which means art, skills and many other meanings. We want people to encourage themselves to do great things with their hands, minds, and imagination.
